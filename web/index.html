Sales Bot

Our Sales Bot will pitch our education coaching center to potential customers. However due to resource constraints there are few things that is needed to follow to keep it operational. Working:-

  • When click first the bot will take some time to respond as it is downloading models in backend.
  • When it finished greeting you can go on and transcribe your voice.
  • Only Stop transcribing when the complete sentence that you want to speak appears in transcribed textbox.
  • When Finished Transcribing please click on the Speaking Circle to let bot process.
  • Bot will take couple of minutes to generate its first respond.
  • After the initial wait time for model downloading it will work without any hiccups and can answer your questions indefinetly.
